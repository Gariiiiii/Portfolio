Major Project's

PGLife

PGLife, a standout platform that makes it easy to find paid guest accommodations in major cities like Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore, and Hyderabad. My involvement in this project focused on enhancing the website's design, interface, and compatibility across different devices, ensuring a seamless experience whether users accessed it on their smartphones or laptops. With a strong emphasis on user satisfaction, I aimed to create an attractive and intuitive interface that would encourage people searching for their ideal living arrangements to explore the options available to them. Ultimately, the aim was to offer consumers a straightforward and enjoyable experience in selecting their perfect place to live.

Technologies Used

HTML5 | CSS3 | Bootstrap | JavaScript | React | MySQL | PHP
